Neon Nights

Welcome to Neon Nights, a thrilling cyberpunk adventure set in a sprawling futuristic metropolis glowing with neon lights and danger lurking around every corner.

Experience a world where technology meets rebellion, and your choices shape the fate of the city.

The Story

In the year 2147, the megacorporations control everything. You play as a renegade hacker aiming to take down the corrupt elite and uncover hidden secrets buried deep beneath the city’s neon haze.

Navigate dark alleys, neon-lit skyscrapers, and underground networks to rewrite the future.

Characters

Vex: The cunning hacker with a mysterious past.

Echo: A street-smart mercenary skilled with cybernetic implants.

Neon: The enigmatic AI guiding you through the city’s secrets.

Gameplay

Explore a dynamic open world filled with hacking challenges, stealth missions, and combat encounters. Customize your cybernetic abilities and upgrade your gear to gain the edge against your enemies.

Choose your path carefully, as every decision impacts the city and your allies.
